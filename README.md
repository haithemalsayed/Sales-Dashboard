# 📊 Sales Dashboard Summary

An interactive Sales Dashboard built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions, including sales, quantity, customers, products, markets, regions, and business segments.

## 📌 Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance for the selected year, region, and product color. It enables users to quickly identify trends, top-performing products, customer performance, and regional sales distribution through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Executive KPI Summary
  - Total Quantity Sold
  - Total Sales
  - Average Sales
  - Maximum Sale
  - Total Orders

- Quarterly Sales Analysis
  - Q1 Sales
  - Q2 Sales
  - Q3 Sales
  - Q4 Sales

- Market Performance
  - Sales by country/market
  - Easy comparison across markets

- Customer Analysis
  - Top 10 Customers
  - Bottom 10 Customers

- Product Analysis
  - Top 10 Categories
  - Top 10 Models

- Sales Distribution
  - Sales by Product Color
  - Sales by Business Segment
  - Sales by Region

- Interactive Filters (Slicers)
  - Year
  - Region
  - Product Color

## 📂 Dashboard Sections

### Executive Summary
Displays the main KPIs for quick business insights.

### Quarterly Sales
Shows sales performance across all four quarters.

### Market Analysis
Compares sales across different countries and markets.

### Customer Insights
Highlights the highest and lowest-performing customers.

### Product Performance
Identifies the best-selling product categories and models.

### Regional Analysis
Visualizes sales distribution by geographic region.

### Business Segment Analysis
Breaks down sales across different business segments.

---

## 🎯 Business Insights

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- Which quarter generated the highest sales?
- Which market performs the best?
- Who are the top customers?
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- How are sales distributed across regions?
- Which product colors sell the most?
